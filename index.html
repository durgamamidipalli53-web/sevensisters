Welcome to Seven Sisters Waterfalls

The Seven Sisters Waterfalls, also known as Nohsngithiang Falls, is one of the most beautiful waterfalls in Meghalaya. It is located near Cherrapunji and is famous for its seven separate streams that flow gracefully down the limestone cliffs.
