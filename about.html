History & Significance

Seven Sisters Waterfalls, also known as Nohsngithiang Falls, is one of the most iconic natural attractions of Meghalaya. Located near Cherrapunji, this magnificent waterfall is formed by seven distinct streams flowing down a steep limestone cliff.

Why It Is Special

The waterfall appears most spectacular during the monsoon season when all seven streams flow together in harmony. It represents the beauty of Meghalaya’s lush green hills, heavy rainfall, and rich natural heritage.
